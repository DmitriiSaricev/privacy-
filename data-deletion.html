How to Request Data Deletion

In accordance with GDPR (EU Regulation 2016/679), you have the right to request complete deletion of your personal data from our systems.

Option 1: In-App Deletion

Send the command /reset to the bot in your messenger (Telegram, Facebook, Instagram, or WhatsApp). This will immediately delete:

  • Your complete chat history
  • Chat summaries
  • Lead records associated with your account
  • Execution logs related to your conversations

Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to dmitriisaricev@gmail.com with subject "Data Deletion Request" and include:

  • Your name
  • The messenger platform you used (Telegram/Facebook/Instagram/WhatsApp)
  • Your username or profile link
Processing time: In-app deletion (/reset) is instant. Email requests are processed within 30 days.

What Data is Deleted

  • Chat history (all messages between you and the bot)
  • Chat summaries (compressed conversation history)
  • Lead information (name, phone, if provided)
  • Execution logs (technical logs of bot processing)

After deletion, no personal data associated with your account remains in our active systems. Backup copies are purged within 30 days.
